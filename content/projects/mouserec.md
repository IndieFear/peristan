## What is MouseRec?

MouseRec is a lightweight macOS utility that helps you automate repetitive tasks by recording your mouse and keyboard actions, then replaying them on demand as reusable macros.

## Key features

- Precise capture of mouse and keyboard actions
- Save sequences as reusable macros
- One‑click automatic playback
- Loop playback to repeat sequences continuously
- Adjustable playback speed (2×, 3×, 5×)
- Global keyboard shortcuts for quick access

## Keyboard shortcuts

MouseRec supports global hotkeys that work system-wide, even when the app is in the background:

- **⌘⇧R** — Start/stop recording
- **⌘⇧P** — Playback the last recorded macro

## Getting started

1. Record a sequence: start recording, perform your actions, then stop.
2. Save your macro and give it a name.
3. Replay it once, loop it, or change the playback speed.
