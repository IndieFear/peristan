# Privacy Policy for MouseRec

**Last updated:** November 12, 2025

## Data Collection

MouseRec does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Local Processing Only

All recording and playback functionality operates entirely on your local Mac. MouseRec:
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not use cloud services
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not require an internet connection to function

## No Tracking

MouseRec does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry features. Your usage of the application remains completely private.

## Permissions

MouseRec requires accessibility permissions to record and replay mouse and keyboard actions. These permissions are:
- Requested only when you start recording
- Used solely for the automation functionality
- Never transmitted or shared outside your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the [MouseRec project page](/projects/mouserec/).
